Learning Outcomes

Upon completing this module, participants will be able to:

  • Develop a shared vocabulary of AI concepts across different roles
  • Distinguish between AI and traditional software systems
  • Identify where AI provides value and where it may not be beneficial
  • Understand practical capabilities and limitations of AI tools
  • Interpret AI-generated outputs critically within clinical context
